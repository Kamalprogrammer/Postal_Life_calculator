Welcome to postallifeinsurance.pro

Postal Life Insurance Calculator (PLI) is a well respected and historic insurance program in India, which was launched back in 1884 to primarily cater to postal employees. Over the years, it has expanded its reach to include various government and semi-government workers. People really appreciate Postal Life Insurance policies for their affordable premiums, bonus returns, and long-term reliability.

postallifeinsurance.pro is our PLI Calculator platform, an independent digital space designed to make understanding and calculating Postal Life Insurance benefits easier. We provide a free, easy-to-use PLI Premium Calculator that allows users to quickly estimate their policy premiums, maturity values, and expected returns in just a few clicks.

We provide detailed information about all major Postal Life Insurance schemes such as: Suraksha (Whole Life Assurance), Santosh (Endowment Assurance), Suvidha (Convertible Assurance), Sumangal (Money Back Policy), Yugal Suraksha (Joint Life Policy), and Bal Jeevan Bima (Children Policy).
